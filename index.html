About Little Kyoto

A Slice of Japan in Cebu

Little Kyoto Cebu is a stunning Japanese-inspired destination located in Busay, Cebu City. This beautiful complex features traditional Japanese architecture, serene gardens, and authentic cultural elements that transport visitors to the heart of Japan.

With its iconic torii gates, cherry blossom trees, wooden bridges, and tranquil koi ponds, Little Kyoto offers a peaceful escape from the bustling city life. It's a perfect spot for photography, relaxation, and experiencing Japanese culture without leaving the Philippines.
